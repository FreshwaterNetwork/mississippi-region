Welcome! This tool can help you view and analyze watershed data. You have 3 options for getting started:

Option 1: Search for a Location (e.g. town,state )
 

Option 2: Click on any HUC8 watershed in the map to view more detailed HUC12 watershed data.

Option 3: View all available HUC12s and make your selections from there.
