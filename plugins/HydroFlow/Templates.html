Welcome to HydroFlows! This tool is designed to provide you with common flow statistics that relay information on current and future water flow trends in Louisiana (View Methods). You have 3 options for getting started:

Option 1

Search for a Location (e.g. town,state )

Option 2:

Click on any HUC8 watershed in the map to view more detailed HUC12 watershed data.

Option 3:

View all available HUC12s and make your selections from there.

Quick Facts:

  1. We modeled all watersheds above tidal influence for both current (1983-2012) and future (2013-2042) conditions.
  2. Future conditions were calculated for three scenarios – climate change, projected water use (by sector), and the combination of climate change and projected water use.
  3. The app returns the Index of Hydrologic Alteration (IHA) flow metrics (Richter et al. 1996). These commonly accepted metrics provide characteristics of flow and flow alteration that influence wetlands, rivers, and other aquatic systems and the organisms, like fish, that live there.
  4. This app uses both the US Geological Survey’s Hydrologic Unit Classification System (HUC) and the National Hydrography Dataset. IHA metrics are shown for HUC-12 sub-watersheds (10,000-40,000 acres (15.6 to 62.5 mi2) and NHDPlus Catchments (average about 1-mi2; km2).
